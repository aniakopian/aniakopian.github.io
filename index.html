ABOUT ME

Ani Akopian

Hello! My name is Ani Akopian and I am from Walpole Massachusetts. I am currently a junior at Wheaton College! I am a business and management major but my concentration is with marketing, new media and media analytics! My goal is to focus on fashion marketing in the future, or honestly anything design based. I have always had a love for art and design which is why I am so excited for this Digital Fabrication course. I design clothes and sculpt in my free time, as well as shoot photos which is why i'm eager to channel those passions into our curriculum.

I have learned a bit from my past media courses on HTML and CSS and I hope my skills can develop throughout making this website this semester! The assignments we have ahead of us will definitely have a positive impact on my skills and resumé for my future in media. I am looking forward to our digital embroidery assignment since most of my experience has only been with a sewing machine, but I believe those skills tie in well with one another. Since marketing and new media are both centered around technology and the digital world, I am excited to see my progression in DigiFab!
